# Japan — when to go
The best times to visit Japan are spring (late March–April) for cherry blossoms
and autumn (October–November) for fall foliage and mild weather. The rainy season
(tsuyu) runs roughly early June to mid-July. Summers are hot and humid; winters are
cold in the north and good for skiing. Late October offers comfortable temperatures
and lower crowds, ideal for food tours and hiking.
